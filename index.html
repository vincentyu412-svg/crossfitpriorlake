Frequently Asked Questions About CrossFit Prior Lake

Everything you need to know before your first class at Prior Lake's top-rated CrossFit gym — from pricing and what to wear, to how we scale workouts for all fitness levels.

Do I need CrossFit experience to join?

Absolutely not. CrossFit Prior Lake welcomes complete beginners every day. Every workout is scaled to your current fitness level by our certified coaches, so you can start from wherever you are — no experience required.

How much does a CrossFit Prior Lake membership cost?

We offer several membership options including unlimited group classes, personal training packages, and drop-in rates. Contact us or book a free intro class to learn about current pricing and find the plan that best fits your goals and budget.

What should I wear to my first CrossFit class?

Wear comfortable athletic clothing and supportive sneakers (cross-trainers or flat-soled shoes work best). Bring a water bottle — we provide everything else you need for your first workout at our Prior Lake gym.

Is CrossFit safe for beginners and older adults?

Yes. CrossFit is infinitely scalable. Our coaches modify every movement to accommodate injuries, mobility limitations, and different fitness levels. We train members from age 14 to 70+ safely and effectively.

What areas does CrossFit Prior Lake serve?

Our gym is located at 17205 Adelmann St SE in Prior Lake, MN 55372. We serve members from Prior Lake, Savage, Shakopee, Burnsville, Lakeville, Jordan, and the broader Scott County area.

What does a typical CrossFit class look like?

Each one-hour class includes a guided warm-up, skill or strength work, a high-intensity workout of the day (WOD), and a cool-down. Classes are led by certified coaches who explain every movement and ensure proper form throughout.
