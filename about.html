Our Story

Built On Community

What started as a small garage gym in 2015 has grown into an 8,000 sq ft state-of-the-art CrossFit facility serving hundreds of members across Prior Lake, Savage, Shakopee, Burnsville, and the greater Scott County area.

CrossFit Prior Lake was founded with a simple mission: create a space where regular people could train like athletes, supported by world-class coaching and a community that feels like family.

Our core hasn't changed — we still treat every member like family, and we still believe that fitness is for everyone, regardless of age, background, or experience level. From first-time gym-goers to competitive athletes, everyone belongs here.

Our coaches bring decades of combined experience in CrossFit, Olympic weightlifting, gymnastics, and nutrition. Every program is designed with purpose, and every class is coached with intention.
