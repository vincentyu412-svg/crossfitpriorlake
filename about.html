Our Story

CrossFit Prior Lake started in 2015 with a simple idea: create a space where regular people could train like athletes, supported by world-class coaching and an unbeatable community.

What began in a small garage has grown into a state-of-the-art 8,000 sq ft facility with hundreds of members. But our core hasn't changed – we still treat every member like family, and we still believe that fitness is for everyone.

Our coaches bring decades of combined experience in CrossFit, Olympic weightlifting, gymnastics, and nutrition. Every program is designed with purpose, and every class is coached with intention.

Our Values

The principles that drive everything we do

Community First

We believe the people you train with matter just as much as the training itself. Our community is our greatest asset.

Relentless Improvement

Good enough is never enough. We constantly evolve our programming, coaching, and facility to serve you better.

Safety & Technique

We prioritize movement quality above all. Heavy weights can wait – good mechanics and consistency come first.

Inclusivity

Every body, every background, every fitness level. If you're willing to work hard, you belong here.
