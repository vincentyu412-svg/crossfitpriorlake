Got Questions?

Membership FAQ

Everything you need to know about joining CrossFit Prior Lake — from experience requirements to cancellation policies.

Do I need experience to join?

Absolutely not. Our Foundations course teaches you everything you need to know. Many of our most dedicated members started with zero fitness background. Every workout is scaled to your level from day one.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts. All memberships are month-to-month. We believe in earning your membership every single day — not locking you into fine print.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes! You can freeze your membership for up to 2 months per year for vacations, injuries, or life events. Just give us 7 days notice and we'll take care of the rest.

What should I bring to class?

Just yourself, water, and athletic clothing. We provide all equipment — barbells, dumbbells, rowers, and everything else. Many members eventually get their own lifting shoes and jump rope, but it's not required to start.

Can I try before I commit?

Absolutely. Your first class is completely free with no obligation. Come experience a workout, meet the coaches, and see the community before making any decisions.
