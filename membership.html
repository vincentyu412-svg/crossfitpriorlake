Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Do I need experience to join?

Absolutely not. Our Foundations course teaches you everything you need to know. Many of our most dedicated members started with zero fitness background.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts. All memberships are month-to-month. We believe in earning your membership every single day.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes! You can freeze your membership for up to 2 months per year for vacations, injuries, or life events. Just give us 7 days notice.

What should I bring to class?

Just yourself, water, and athletic clothing. We provide all equipment. Many members eventually get their own lifting shoes and jump rope, but it's not required.
