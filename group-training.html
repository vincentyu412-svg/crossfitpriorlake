What Is Group Training?

Our Group Training (CrossFit) program is the heart of CrossFit Prior Lake. Every class is led by an expert coach who guides you through a structured warm-up, skill work, and a Workout of the Day (WOD) designed to challenge every fitness level.

Whether you're a seasoned athlete or stepping into a gym for the first time, every movement is scalable so you can work at your own pace while still pushing your limits.

What To Expect

  • Coach-led classes with expert guidance on every movement
  • Warm-up, skill/strength work, and a WOD every session
  • All movements scaled to your ability level
  • A supportive community that cheers you on
  • Workouts that build strength, speed, and endurance
  • No experience required — beginners welcome
