Pick 3–4 Proteins

"Pick 3" means 3 servings — not 3 different items. Love chicken? Just multiply the quantity on the grocery list by 3 (e.g. 3 lbs × 3 = 9 lbs). Or pick a different protein for breakfast, lunch, and dinner to keep it easy and varied.

Sunday meal prep: Cook your proteins and carbs in bulk, chop your veggies, and portion into containers. One hour on Sunday saves you all week.

Durations are suggestions — keep what works, swap when you want variety.

Your 4-Week Progression
