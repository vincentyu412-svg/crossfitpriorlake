Got Questions?

Personal Training FAQ

Everything you need to know about one-on-one coaching at CrossFit Prior Lake.

How is personal training different from group classes?

Personal training gives you 100% of your coach's attention for the entire session. Programming is built entirely around your goals, schedule, and ability — whereas group classes follow a shared workout of the day. Both are coached by certified professionals.

How many sessions per week do I need?

Most clients see excellent results with 2–3 sessions per week. Your coach will recommend a frequency based on your goals, recovery capacity, and whether you're also attending group classes. We build the plan around your life, not the other way around.

Can I combine personal training with a group membership?

Absolutely — and many of our members do exactly that. Personal training sessions complement group classes perfectly, letting you work on specific weaknesses or goals with individualized coaching while still enjoying the community energy of group WODs.

What if I have an injury or physical limitation?

Personal training is ideal for working around injuries. Your coach designs every session with your limitations in mind, coordinating with your healthcare provider when needed. We focus on safe, progressive movement to rebuild strength and confidence.

How much does personal training cost?

Personal training sessions start at $80/hour. We offer single sessions and discounted packages. Book a free consultation to discuss your goals and we'll recommend the best option for your situation — no commitment required.
